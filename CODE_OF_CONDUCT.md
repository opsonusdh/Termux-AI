# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in **Termux-AI** a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, identity, or skill level.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project
- Helping others learn without mocking them for asking questions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling, baiting, or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Personal attacks in issues, pull requests, or discussions
- Behavior that would reasonably be considered unprofessional or hostile

---

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including:
- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- project-related chats
- other official communication channels

---

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainer directly through GitHub.

All complaints will be reviewed and handled fairly and promptly.
